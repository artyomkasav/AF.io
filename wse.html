Why is Archive Fashion So Expensive?

♪ Music ♪

Limited Production

Many archive pieces were produced in extremely limited quantities, making them rare collector's items.

Historical Value

Some pieces define entire fashion eras, influencing future designs and carrying historical weight.

Materials and Craftsmanship

Many archival pieces use premium materials and feature handcrafted details, increasing their cost.

Cultural Influence

Worn by celebrities and featured in pop culture, these pieces gain legendary status and demand.
